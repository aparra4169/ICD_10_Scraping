N18.1 Chronic kidney disease, stage 1
Definition
Kidney damage with normal or increased GFR (≥ 90 mL/min)
N18.2 Chronic kidney disease, stage 2
Definition

Definition
Kidney damage with moderately decreased GFR (30-59 mL/min)
